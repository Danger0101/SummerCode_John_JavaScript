## Intro
ParentHood is a dedicated space for those with children who want to explore their local area, meet other likeminded parents and make new, lasting connections. To support with depression and anxiety, the aite supports activities which encourage socialisation, exploring the local community, being apart of nature, undertaking low impact exercise for example, walking, and creating connections and networks.

The site allows for users to find events which interst them, submit an event for others to join and provides a space to chat and get to know each other.

All parents welcome, including GrandParents and children of all ages are welcome.
